About Princess Bubblegum

Princess Bonnibel "Bonnie" Bubblegum, commonly known as Princess Bubblegum, is the benevolent ruler of the Candy Kingdom. With her intelligence and scientific prowess, she oversees the well-being of her candy citizens whom she created years decades before. She is also a key figure in the adventures or quests that Finn and Jake often participate it, usually providing aid or seeking their help and protection. In the earlier seasons, this would involve needing rescue from the Ice King, an old ice wizard residing in the Ice Kingdom.
