Fun Facts about Princess Bubblegum

  • 1.) Princess Bubblegum is the reincarnation of the Candy Elemental; she can shoot jelly beans out of her hands.
  • 2.) Princess Bubblegum's age is over 800 years due to her regenerative abilities.
  • 3.) Princess Bubblegum's birthday is on October 16. Although, the exact year when she was born was never clarified.
