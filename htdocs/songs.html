Songs including Princess Bubblegum:

Princess Bubblegum has been associated and included in many songs across the duration of the show. Here are some notable songs in which she appears:

  • "Everything Stays" - Featured in season 7 ep 7 of the Adventure Time miniseries "Stakes," this song reflects on the eternal aspects of life; how everything is permanent, and at the same time, temporary.
  • "My Best Friends in the World" - Princess Bubblegum sings this song along with Finn, Jake, and Marceline in the episode "What Was Missing."
  • "Monster" - Plays in season 1 ep 2 of "Adventure Time: Distant Lands", a love song composed by Marceline dedicated to Princess Bubblegum.
