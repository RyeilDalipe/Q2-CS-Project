Princess Bubblegum's Relationships

Princess Bubblegum has complex relationships with various characters in the Land of Ooo. Here are some of the key characters she interacts with:

  • Finn the Human - A good friend to Princess Bubblegum, but is also sometimes treated as a test subject. Despite his feelings and attempts to woo her, Princess Bubblegum has rejected all of Finn's advances.
  • Jake the Dog - Jake is also a friend to Princess Bubblegum, although sometimes she treats him more like a pet rather than an equal; an act that Jake seems to like taking part in.
  • Marceline the Vampire Queen - Their complex relationship has made for some interesting episodes and subplots. In the later seasons, it is revealed that they have a lot of history together, and were implied to have been exes. Now, they are confirmed to be in a relationship by the creators.
